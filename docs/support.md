## Common Questions

### How do I set up reminders?
Open Pillie and follow the onboarding flow to configure your routine schedule and reminder times.

### How do I change my reminder times?
Go to your routine settings within the app to adjust your schedule and notification preferences.

### Why am I not receiving notifications?
Make sure notifications are enabled for Pillie in your device Settings > Notifications > Pillie. Also check that Do Not Disturb or Focus modes are not blocking notifications.

### How do I delete my data?
All your data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app will remove all associated data.

### How does app blocking work?
If enabled, Pillie uses Screen Time to help you stay focused during your routine. You can manage which apps are blocked in the app settings.

## Report an Issue

If you've found a bug or have a feature request, email us at [pillieapp@gmail.com](mailto:pillieapp@gmail.com) with a description of the issue and your device model and iOS version.
