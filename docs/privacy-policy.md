# Pillie Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 20, 2026

## 1. Overview
Pillie ("Pillie," "we," "our," or "us") is a lifestyle reminder app designed to help users stay consistent with daily routines. This Privacy Policy explains what information is processed when you use Pillie and what choices you have.

## 2. Information We Process
Pillie is designed to keep your routine data on your device.

Information stored on your device may include:
- Reminder settings (time and interval preferences)
- Routine setup and schedule preferences
- Completion history and streak-related data
- App-blocking selections (if enabled in app settings)

We do not require account creation to use Pillie.

## 3. Notifications
If you allow notifications, Pillie schedules local notifications on your device to remind you about your routine. Notification content and related reminder metadata are managed locally.

## 4. Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal information.
We do not share your routine data with third parties for advertising.

We may disclose information only if required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security.

## 5. Third-Party Platforms and Services
Pillie is distributed through Apple platforms (such as the App Store and TestFlight). Apple may collect information under Apple's own privacy policies. Pillie does not control Apple's data practices.

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion
Your in-app routine data is stored locally on your device and remains there until you delete it (for example, by resetting the app data or uninstalling the app).

## 7. Children
Pillie is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us and we will address the request.

## 8. International Users
If you use Pillie outside Canada, you understand that your use is subject to applicable local laws. Where required, we will take steps to comply with relevant data protection obligations.

## 9. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above.
